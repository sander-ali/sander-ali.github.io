I am delighted to share that I have been awarded the Outstanding Research Award from the [Multimedia Computing Systems Laboratory (MCSLab)](http://mcsl.skku.edu/) at [Sungkyunkwan University](https://www.skku.edu/), South Korea. This recognition acknowledges the significant contributions and dedication to research excellence in mobile computing systems.

The award, presented by MCSLab, one of the premier research laboratories at Sungkyunkwan University, reflects the commitment to advancing mobile computing technologies and systems through innovative research approaches. This achievement serves as both validation of past work and motivation for future research endeavors.
