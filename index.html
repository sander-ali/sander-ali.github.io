I am currently based in South Korea, employed as Postdoctoral Research Fellow at Department of Mechatronics, Korea Polytechnic University. I received by Ph.D. degree from Department of Industrial and Information Systems Engineering, Hankuk University of Foreign Studies, South Korea. I completed my Masters and Bachelors from Mehran University of Engineering and Technology, Jamshoro. I served as Assistant Professor, Lecturer, Network Engineer-Projects, and System Support Engineer in different national and multi-national companies. My current research interests are deep learning for computer vision, data- and model-security, and emerging technologies.
